I. Choose the response which means the same as the underlined word.

1. The students were advised to discover their innate abilities.
a. creative b. natural c. intelligent d. abnormal

2. The boss and her management team often vituperated their employees.
a. scolded b. praised c. honored d. discouraged

3. One of the finalists was praised for his knowledge; the other for his ingenuity.
a. hard work b. perseverance c. cleverness d. honor

a. stinking b. mild c. sweet d. strong

5. The friend surprised her group with her indolence.
a. laziness b. strength c. intelligence d. attitude

6. Loquacious people may be difficult to deal with on a daily basis.
a. irate b. talkative c. inventive d. exasperating

7. The movie caused trepidation in its watchers much to everyone's surprise.
a. shock b. sadness c. fear d. disbelief

8. An infelicitous remark may have disastrous consequences.
a. unfortunate b. provocative c. inappropriate d. intelligent

9. The girl and her friends decided to subvert the typical stereotypes.
a. promote b. overthrow c. help d. drive

10. Everyone performed their duties that day with a bit of alacrity.
a. laziness b. strength c. readiness d. trust
