I. Choose the response which means the same as the underlined word.

1. The classicists are studying the nascent civilization of ancient Greece.
a. intelligent b. advanced c. creative d. beginning

2. The answer was conjectured from the facts given in the paper.
a. guessed b. proven c. assimilated d. proven false

3. A sedentary lifestyle has been linked to a higher risk of heart disease.
a. mobile b. itinerant c. inactive d. thrill-seeking

4. The defendant’s rule of lenity argument was deemed meritorious.
a. clemency b. ambiguity c. negligence d. torts

5. After years of obloquy from her father, the girl was glad to move in with her
mother and step-father.
a. rigidity b. verbal abuse c. neglect d. permissiveness

6. Pliny the Elder wrote extensively about horticulture in the 1st century C.E.
a. history b. philosophy c. gardening d. arts

7. The boys were embarrassed by the profuse laudatory comments by their
teacher.
a. scant b. excessive c. inappropriate d. inopportune

8. It was incumbent upon the president to react very quickly to the threat of such
an attack.
a. necessary for b. reckless for c. impossible for d. credible for

9. They presented a cogent argument for the passage of the constitutional
amendment.
a. flimsy b. serious c. fallacious d. compelling

10. Although he was somnambulant, he never seemed to get into trouble.
a. defiant b. an overeater c. a wanderer d. a sleepwalker
